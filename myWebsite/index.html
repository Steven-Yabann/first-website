FRIED EGGS

Ingredients

  • 4 fresh large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter/oil
  • Salt and freshly-ground black pepper

Method

  1. When frying more than one egg, add all eggs at once
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet (or a regular skillet greased with a small amount of butter or cooking oil) at medium heat until just hot enough to sizzle a drop of water. Or use a regular skillet and add a small amount of butter or oil
  3. Break eggs into a bowl and gently slip into the skillet
  4. Reduce heat to low, cook slowly until whites are completely set and yolks begin to thicken but are not hard
  5. Season with salt and pepper as desired
  6. Garnish and serve with choice of bread
